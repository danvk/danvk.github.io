QuickTime SMPTE effects

  These are the 133 standard effects, as stated by the SMPTE (Society of Motion Picture & Television Engineers). The final five effects are ones that Apple has added on their own. There are many other effects that apple has made (such as flaming transitions), but RB does not provide access to them yet (they're identified by a four letter code, not a number).

  The SMPTE effects are divided into four sections: Wipes, Irises, Radials, and Matrices.
