Last Update: Thursday April 11, 1999

  RB Monthly is a monthly (more or less) magazine about REALbasic. It contains Tips, Reviews, Tutorials, and all the other things that make a magazine a magazine.

Announcements

  OK, all of the issues are now uploaded in stuffed format. Four issues are now available in PDF format. Also new is the compilations page, which contains lists and compilations of every RB release since DR1r4, all of our reviews and articles, as well as Tips and Quick Tips.

  I'm looking for writers. Specifically, anyone who's willing to write a tutorial/review on a monthly basis. If you think about it, this is very, very little work. It amounts to writing a page or two on RB each month. And, as a bonus, if you write, RB Monthly will be a better magazine, and it'll be easier for me to create—something that will ensure its continued existence. If you're interested, send some e-mail to dantheox@aol.com.

  The response to this has been pretty good so far, but more is always better!

Older Issues

  RB Monthly was started in June 1998. Since then, there have been several issues, with a new one (roughly) each month. All of them are online. Click the month or cover of an issue to view it. Click (Stuffit) after the month to download the issue for viewing on your hard drive. Click (PDF) if you want a PDF version of the issue. These aren't available for all issues yet. Yes, these issues aren't the latest, so the not all the news is relevant, but the tips, tutorials, and reviews that they contain are all still very useful.
