CanvasPaint 1.1

  CanvasPaint is a class that allows you to add excellent painting capabilities to your programs. Currently, CanvasPaint supports the following:

  • Ten tools, including Selections, spray cans, lines, pencils, etc.
  • Built-in scrolling
  • Selections
  • Zooming
  • Saving in a variety of formats

  In the future, CanvasPaint will also include antialiasing, and undoing.

  As it is, it's definitely a nifty product, and it comes with full documentation and an example project. Now don't you feel obliged to download...
