LingoScript

  LingoScript is a module that provides scripting capabilities to realbasic. This means that you can allow users of your program to write their own programs (scripts) from in your app. This allows you to give users of your program lots of power over it.

  If you're going to include LingoScript in your program, here's what you need to do:

  • Mention me in your ReadMe (Dan Vanderkam), and put a reference to my LingoScript wxeb page (www.nd.edu/~jvanderk/rb/lingoscript.html) in it.
  • Include a "This app uses LingoScript graphic". (Optional).
  • Tell me that the program uses LingoScript (so that I can add it to a list). Give me your name, e-mail address, and the program's URL. Also, give me a URL that contains a picture of the program's icon.
  • If it makes extensive use of it, you should give me a free copy.
